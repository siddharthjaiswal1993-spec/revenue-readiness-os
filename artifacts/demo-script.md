# Demo Script — Revenue Readiness OS

---

## Setup

**Persona:** Aanya Mehta, VP Sales Enablement, mid-market B2B SaaS company, 120 quota-carrying reps, has been in role 8 months, inherited a fragmented training program, struggling to show enablement ROI to her CRO (Dev Malhotra).

**The narrative hook:** "Aanya was asked last week to present enablement ROI at the board meeting. Her team spent the weekend pulling data from three tools and a spreadsheet. She still couldn't answer Dev's question: 'Are our reps actually getting better at winning?'"

---

## 5-Minute Demo

**[0:00 – 0:45] Open on Command Center**

> "This is the Revenue Readiness OS command center. The moment Aanya logs in Monday morning, she sees the thing she couldn't get from three tools: which reps are ready to win this week."

- Point to the overall org Readiness Index: 64/100 (Developing)
- Point to the "At Risk" and "Critical" rep count: 8 reps need attention
- Point to the 30-day trend line: score is up 4 points since last month
- Quick stat: "12 coaching actions were completed this week. 3 are overdue."

> "Instead of pulling a report, she has her answer before her first meeting."

**[0:45 – 2:00] Coaching Queue — Manager View**

> "Switch to Rohan Shah's view — he manages the Enterprise AE team. Here's his coaching queue for the week."

- Show 3 coaching actions: Kavya Rao (Discovery — Critical), Arjun Nair (Objection Handling — High), one more rep (Product Knowledge — Medium)
- Click into Kavya's action:
  - Evidence: "Roleplay session score: 42/100 on Discovery. Key gap: not asking about current state before proposing solution. 2 of 3 recent call transcripts show same pattern."
  - Suggested approach: "In your 1:1 Thursday, use the SPIN questioning framework. Ask Kavya to narrate her current discovery flow — listen for where she jumps to solution."

> "Rohan didn't have to find this. He didn't have to pull Kavya's scores, read her call transcripts, or remember what they discussed last month. It's ready on Monday morning, grounded in evidence."

**[2:00 – 3:15] AI Roleplay — Rep View**

> "Now let's see what Kavya saw before that score landed on Rohan's desk."

- Switch to Kavya's view; open a Discovery roleplay scenario
- Show the buyer persona: "Jordan, VP of Operations at a mid-market logistics company, evaluating supply chain tools"
- Play a brief exchange (3–4 turns)
- Open the debrief report:
  - Score: 42/100
  - Strength: "Strong rapport-building in the opener"
  - Gap 1: "Jumped to product features after first pain statement; did not probe root cause or scope"
  - Gap 2: "Did not ask about current solution or what is working before proposing change"
  - Transcript evidence highlighted

> "Kavya practiced at 10pm with no scheduling required. The debrief told her exactly what to work on. And Rohan knew about it by Monday."

**[3:15 – 4:15] Readiness Score Trend**

> "Back to Aanya. She needs to show Dev ROI. Here's how."

- Navigate to Analytics → Readiness Trend
- Show the correlation chart: teams with RRI ≥ 70 show a 6-point higher win rate on competitive deals
- Show the ramp cohort chart: reps who completed ≥3 roleplay sessions in month 1 ramped 7 weeks faster than those who did not

> "This is the answer to Dev's question. Not training completions. Skill improvement to revenue outcomes."

**[4:15 – 5:00] Close**

> "Revenue Readiness OS gives Aanya a system of record for the skill layer of her revenue organization. Reps practice. Managers coach. The data compounds. And the CRO finally has an answer."

---

## 10-Minute Demo (Extended)

Add the following sections after the 5-minute script:

**[5:00 – 6:30] Content Hub**

> "One more problem Aanya's team faces: 60% of their content never gets used. Reps can't find it. Here's what changes."

- Open Content Hub
- Show search: type "competitive objection enterprise"
- Show top 3 results: battle card (freshness score: 87), case study (freshness: 62), FAQ (freshness: 34)
- Point out freshness scores: "The FAQ is stale. Aanya gets a notification to refresh it."
- Switch to a deal in the Deal Readiness page: "For Kavya's TechCorp deal, AI has automatically recommended these three assets based on deal stage, buyer persona, and the last call signals."

> "The right content, for the right deal, without the rep having to search."

**[6:30 – 8:00] Program Effectiveness**

> "Aanya just launched a new discovery skills program. Four weeks in, here's what she sees."

- Navigate to Programs → Discovery Excellence Program
- Show completion rate: 78% (6 reps not started)
- Show skill score change: team Discovery score up from 51 to 58 in 4 weeks
- AI insight card: "Reps who completed Module 3 (SPIN Practice) showed 12-point Discovery improvement vs. 4-point improvement for those who skipped it. Recommend making Module 3 mandatory."

> "The platform tells her not just what happened, but what to do about it."

**[8:00 – 9:15] Executive View**

> "Dev Malhotra logs in once a week. Here's his view."

- Switch to CRO executive dashboard
- Show: Org RRI 64/100 (up 7 points this quarter)
- Show: Average ramp time for Q1 hires: 7.8 months vs. 9.2 months same cohort last year
- Show: Win rate correlation chart
- Show: Top 3 skill gaps across the org (Competitive Positioning, Pricing Confidence, Closing Discipline)

> "Dev has his board answer. Enablement is working. These are the gaps to close next quarter."

**[9:15 – 10:00] Close + Q&A Setup**

> "We've seen four things in 10 minutes: a manager who knows exactly who to coach and why. A rep who practiced and got specific feedback. A VP who can show program effectiveness. And a CRO who has his ROI answer. That's Revenue Readiness OS."

Transition: "What would be most useful to explore more — the AI roleplay experience, the coaching queue, or the analytics?"
