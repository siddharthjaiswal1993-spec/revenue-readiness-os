# Revenue Readiness OS — One-Pager

---

## The Problem

B2B revenue organizations hire fast and train inconsistently. The result: long ramp times, unpredictable win rates, and expensive coaching that relies on manager instinct rather than skill data.

- Average AE ramp time: 9–12 months
- Average manager coaching prep: 2–4 hours per week
- Average content utilization: <30% of assets ever viewed
- Training ROI: measured by completion rate, not skill improvement

**The root cause:** Revenue organizations lack a system of record for skill development. They have tools for deals (CRM), calls (call intelligence), and documents (content management) — but no equivalent infrastructure for the skill layer that determines whether reps can actually win.

---

## The Solution

**Revenue Readiness OS** is an AI-native platform that continuously measures rep readiness across 10 skill dimensions, surfaces coaching priorities for managers, enables practice at scale through AI roleplay, and connects skill development to revenue outcomes.

**What makes it different:**

| Traditional approach | Revenue Readiness OS |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| Training completion metrics | Skill signal scoring |
| Manager intuition on who to coach | AI-prioritized coaching queue |
| Scheduled human roleplays | On-demand AI roleplay with scored debrief |
| Content library with search | AI-recommended content by deal context |
| Annual ramp analysis | Real-time ramp progress by rep |

---

## Core Capabilities

**Readiness Intelligence** — Live skill scores per rep across 10 dimensions (discovery, value articulation, product knowledge, competitive positioning, objection handling, demo storytelling, executive communication, closing discipline, security review handling, pricing confidence). Composite readiness score 0–100 with trend data.

**AI Roleplay** — Unlimited scenario-based practice with a realistic AI buyer. Session debrief with overall score, per-skill feedback, and specific transcript evidence. Manager can review all direct report sessions.

**Coaching Queue** — AI generates a prioritized coaching action list per manager each week. Every recommendation is grounded in evidence (roleplay score, assessment result, call signal). Managers can accept, override, or dismiss with one tap.

**Content Hub** — Searchable library with AI-powered recommendations by deal stage, buyer persona, and competitive context. Freshness scoring and usage analytics to keep library alive.

**Analytics** — Readiness score trends by cohort, team, and individual. Program effectiveness attribution. Win rate correlation analysis.

---

## Who It Is For

**VP Sales Enablement** — measures program effectiveness, drives skill improvement across the organization, and proves ROI to the CRO.

**Sales Manager** — prepares for 1:1s with a ready coaching brief, monitors team risk, tracks ramp progress for new hires.

**AE / SDR** — practices without scheduling a human, knows exactly what gaps to close, finds the right content for each deal.

## Business Value

- **Reduce ramp time** by 2+ months for new AEs
- **Improve win rate** by building consistent skills across the team, not just the stars
- **Save manager time** — 2+ hours per week on coaching prep
- **Prove enablement ROI** with readiness-to-revenue correlation data
